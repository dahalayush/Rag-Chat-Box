Nepal is a landlocked country in South Asia, situated between India and China. Known for its diverse geography, Nepal is home to the world's highest mountain, Mount Everest. The country's capital is Kathmandu. Nepal is rich in cultural heritage, with many ethnic groups, languages, traditions, and historic temples. Tourism, agriculture, remittances, and hydropower are important contributors to its economy. Nepal is also recognized as the birthplace of Siddhartha Gautama and is celebrated for its natural beauty, biodiversity, and warm hospitality.
